## Proven Optimization Methods {{ anchor: false }}

Research testing over 10,000 search prompts identified which methods most improve AI visibility:

<Row>
  <Col>
    ### Quotes & Statistics
    
    Adding citations, quotes, and statistics can improve visibility by **30-40%**. AI systems prioritize citable, evidence-backed content.
    
    → [Implementation guide](/strategies/quotes-statistics)
  </Col>
  <Col>
    ### Wikipedia Presence
    
    Wikipedia has **5x weight** in training data and accounts for **~10%** of AI citations. Build eligibility and leverage this critical platform.
    
    → [Wikipedia strategy](/strategies/wikipedia)
  </Col>
</Row>

<Row>
  <Col>
    ### Reddit Engagement
    
    Reddit is the **#1 cited domain** in AI search (~32% of citations). Reddit content is licensed for LLM training. Authentic participation matters.
    
    → [Reddit strategy](/strategies/reddit)
  </Col>
  <Col>
    ### Technical Foundation
    
    Schema.org markup, llms.txt, and proper crawler access ensure AI systems can find and understand your content.
    
    → [Technical guide](/optimization/schema)
  </Col>
</Row>
